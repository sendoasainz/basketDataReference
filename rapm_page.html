PLAYER RAPM – LBA | ACB | Euroleague | Eurocup | BCL

RAPM stands for Regularized Adjusted Plus-Minus. It could be thought of as an improved version of the standard on/off stats: unlike the latter, RAPM takes into account the score differential and the ten players on the court, weighting the score differential, teammates, and opponents. The resulting metric estimates the player’s offensive and defensive contribution (and therefore the overall impact) while he is on the court. RAPM is a seasonal metric, so the results are displayed by league and season.

LEGEND AND FORMULAS

GP: games played
MIN: minutes played

TM POSS: team possessions played

OFF RAPM: offensive Regularized Adjusted Plus-Minus. The greater the value, the better the player’s impact
DEF RAPM: defensive Regularized Adjusted Plus-Minus. The greater the value, the better the player’s impact
NET RAPM: Regularized Adjusted Plus-Minus (OFF+DEF). The greater the value, the better the player’s impact
